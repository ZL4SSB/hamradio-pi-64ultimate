# Propagation

The Propagation page downloads live operational data from the NOAA Space
Weather Prediction Center.

## Live readings

- Solar radio flux
- Planetary Kp
- Solar-wind speed
- Solar-wind density
- Interplanetary Bz
- GOES soft X-ray class

## HF band estimates

Band-condition labels are intentionally marked as estimates. They are derived
from solar flux, Kp and X-ray activity and are a quick guide rather than a
point-to-point propagation prediction.

The page refreshes every five minutes.
